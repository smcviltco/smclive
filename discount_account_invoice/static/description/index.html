Odoo Discounts On Invoices And Bills

By default, you can only add a percentage discount on invoice and bill lines. So the module adds new discount types to them. We have added a FIXED DISCOUNT Type for invoice & bill lines. And We bring GLOBAL DISCOUNT CATEGORY which can be fixed or percent type and apply on the entire invoice or bill.

Now Set Various DIscount Types On Invoices & Bills

The module facilitates the Odoo Users to set discounts in Invoices and bills in Odoo.

  • You can give discounts as Fixed / flat discounts or Percent discounts in Invoices and Bills.
  • The discounts can be applied on the invoice lines or globally on the entire invoice.
  • Also, the discounts can be applied on the bill lines or globally on the entire bill.
  • The discount can be applied on taxed or untaxed amount in the invoices and bills.

Manage discounts in Invoices in Odoo

Add Fixed Discount On Invoice Lines Of A Sales invoice

Note: By default Odoo Only Allow Percent Discount in Invoice Line.

  • But You can now add a flat/fixed discount in the invoice line while creating an invoice in Odoo with the module.

Set Global Fixed Or percent Based Discount on the whole Invoice

Note: By default, Odoo does not facilitate Global Discount On Invoices.

  • With Odoo Discounts On invoices and Bills Module, you can now set Global Discounts in the Invoice while creating Invoices in Odoo.
  • Global Discount can be of Fixed / flat discount or Percent Discount applicable on the total amount of the invoice.

Manage discounts in Bills in Odoo

Set A Fixed or Flat Discount Type In Bill lines In Odoo

Note: In Default Odoo, you can only add percent discount on bill lines.

  • Now you can add a fixed discount on each bill line while creating Bills in Odoo.
  • The discount can be given on Taxed or untaxed amount of the product.

Give Global discount On the Purchase Bills In Odoo

Note: Odoo Does not allow you to add any global discount on the whole Bill.

  • With the module, you can also add a global discount to the purchase bill i.e. give a discount on the whole bill amount.
  • The discount can be fixed or percent based.
  • The discount can be given on taxed or untaxed bill amounts.

Global Discount Applied in the Invoice & Bill Form in Odoo

Global Discount Applied in the Invoice & Bill Form in Odoo

  • Odoo 13 does not support a field that shows global discounts in the invoice and Bill.
  • But Our module shows the glocal discount value in the subtotal on the Invoice and Bill Form.
  • The discount fields are clearly shown to the user. They are also included while printing the bill or invoice.
