Odoo WhatsApp Integration

Customer relationship management (CRM) manages your business's single most important goal - satisfying your customers. WhatsApp is an immensely popular chatting app used by 1.5 Billion people worldwide. It has an easy interface and can be used powerfully with Odoo. Dynexcel has developed an Odoo app which allows users to use the WhatsApp Application to send messages via Odoo. We can send messages from Contacts, Sales, Accounts invoice, Accounts Payments, Delivery orders,Purchase orders. Let us have a look at how this works inside Odoo.

Odoo WhatsApp Integration - Operations

  • Attach And Save Multiple/Unlimited WhatsApp Accounts.
  • Send Messages Along Multiple Attachments To Specific Or Multiple Contacts/Customers From WhatsApp Dashboard.
  • Attach Company Signature Along WhtasApp Messages.
  • Detail Logs Of Each Message Sent To Any Contact/Customer.
  • Flexibility: WhatsApp Message From Contact, Sales, Invoice, Inventory, Purchase And Employee Modules.
  • Send Quotations, SaleOrders, Invoices, Receipts, Payments, Bills, Refunds, Credit Notes Etc To Specific Or Mulitple Customers On Just One Click.
  • Auto Message Along Information While Sending Quotations, SaleOrders, Invoices, Receipts, Payments, Bills, Refunds, Credit Notes Etc To Specific Or Mulitple Customers.
  • Maintain History In Chatter Of Messages Sent Along Files And Company Signature.
  • WhatsApp Counters On Every Transection Are Maintained.
  • WhatsApp Chatter On Every Transection Are Maintained.
  • Reporting: Analysis View To Research And Maintain Company Statistics Accordingly.
  • Channels: Received Messages Are Maintained In Discuss > Channels (With The Name of Contact/Customer/Vendor.

WhatsApp From Contacts - Operations

  • Open Any Contact, Click WhatsApp Button And Send Message.
  • Select Multiple Recipients From List View And Send Message From Odoo Actions.
  • Add Or Remove Recipients On Run Time While Sending Messages.
  • Message History maintain in Chatter Of Every Contact.
  • Detailed Logs In WhatsApp Reporting Section.
  • Counters Against Every Contact
  • Message History Will Be Maintained In Odoo Channels

Is this app compatible with Odoo Enterprise as well as Community?

Yes, the app works perfectly well with Odoo Enterprise (On-premise and Odoo.SH) as well as Community. Odoo Online (Cloud) does not allow installation of third-party apps and hence this app cannot be installed on Odoo Online.
